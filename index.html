METAR Timing Advantage

Weather Underground updates from official METAR observations. Most traders use weather apps that lag 15–45 minutes. First to see the new reading has the edge.

The Rounding Blind Spot

WU rounds to whole degrees. When the actual temp is within 0.5°F of a bracket boundary, most market makers don't account for the rounding mechanics. That's your edge.
